# Forest Diorama Models

The GLB silhouettes in this folder are selected from Quaternius CC0 packs distributed through Poly Pizza:

- `storybook-cottage.glb`: Cabin by Poly by Google, licensed CC BY 3.0 and distributed through Poly Pizza.
- `storybook-pine.glb`: Stylized Nature MegaKit, Pine.
- `storybook-broadleaf.glb`: Stylized Nature MegaKit, dense Tree variant.
- `storybook-broadleaf-irregular.glb`: Stylized Nature MegaKit, irregular Tree variant.
- `storybook-flowering-bush.glb`: Stylized Nature MegaKit, Bush with Flowers.

Sources:

- https://poly.pizza/bundle/Medieval-Village-Pack-NsHhjhlrfY
- https://poly.pizza/bundle/Stylized-Nature-MegaKit-T34GZFA0fm
- https://poly.pizza/m/dTSrDa0oz0a
- https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/
- https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

The production scene applies project-owned hand-painted surface textures and lighting adjustments on top of these silhouettes.
